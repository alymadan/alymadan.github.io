About Me

I graduated from Johns Hopkins University with a B.A. in International Studies and minors in Entrepreneurship and Management and Spanish for the Professions. I have been fortunate enough to work across multiple industries with people of different backgrounds, cultures, and perspectives.

My passion for organizations that focus on addressing and unraveling social issues stems from experiences as an employee, volunteer, and consultant for various nonprofits. These opportunities have sparked my desire to work with start-ups that are finding, analyzing, and solving problems in their own communities and around the world.

I combine analytical know-how with a real passion for creative solutions. Through experience working across industries and leading cross-functional teams, I understand how moving parts fit together while maintaining a close eye on the details.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, travelling, eating good food, photography, music, sailing, and playing Ultimate.
